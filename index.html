YanChoo Yeo

Lighting Consultant of Light Directions

Yanchoo joined Light Directions Singapore (also known as Illuminate Lighting Design before rebranding in 2019) since 2010. With an architectural background, she is self taught in the lighting field and picked up her skill set on the job. Over the years she has worked on international projects in Asia, Middle East and Europe, with accolades on award winning projects. She is currently an associate with Light Directions and leads a team of designers in the Singapore office.
